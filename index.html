About Us

Honest Scribbles works to help you get your novel/short story reviewed by real readers. A one of a kind reviewing platform designed for new writers who are struggling with getting their stories published.

Facing rejections is challenging, specially when you can't figure out where's the fault in your stars.

Doubts surpass your efforts, as you can't tell how well your book will do in the market. Well, what can be better than getting a review with a reader who has actually purchased and read books matching the genre of your creation. That's why, we help you get connected with enthusiastic readers, who can answer all your queries. They'll read your work thoroughly and will give you the best possible honest opinion. Their valuable inputs might help you get ahead of the dead end and help you understand the demand of the audience as well.

  • Pick from a vast number of readers, from any age group, from vetern readers to newbies of the reading world, as per your need.
  • Get all your queries answered in on go.
  • 100% secure reviewing process.

How the process works?

You will be asked to fill up a simple form, stating the genre, word count and other necessary details of your book along with your specifications for the reviewer (ex. age, minimum number of books read. etc.). We will filter out the reviewers and the ones matching your needs would be informed. Upon a update on their availabilty and pricing demands, we'd inform you about all the reviewers available. You then, can pick one or multiple of them by glossing over their reading-details that we'll be sending along.

Once everything is finalized, an agreement would be forged between you and Honest Scribbles. Also, the reviewer would sign a non-disclosure agreement, that would protect your work. The reviewer would submit the review, which would only be available to you. Note that no where in the process, the reviewer would be in possesion of your work. Also, the NDA agreement signed by the reviewer would forbid him/her from taking any piece of material and using it in future.

How the pricing works?

We provide freedom to all the reviewers to pick their own pricing index (usually done on a word count basis). Thus, the cost of getting reviewed varies from reviewer to reviewer.

But an estimated cost for getting a novel of 50k words reviewed ranges from INR750 to INR3000.

You always have a choice over picking the reviewer. There would be no external influence on your decision from our side.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will my work be secure?

    Yes, absolutely. The reviewer would sign an agreement which will legally forbid him/her from using your creation.

  • How can I trust my reviewer?

    You will recieve all the information there is available with us, which will include the number of books they've read, their favourite authors, and few paragraphs worth of their opinion on best and worst of books they've read.

  • How can I trust that I will recieve a review?

    You would be asked to pay only after the reviewer has generated a review. Thus, ensuring that the end-deal on both sides is met.

  • As a reader, how can I get started?

    Fill in the reader form given above. You'd be asked a few questions about your reading habits. Our team will take a look at it and intimate you, whether you are selected or not. After selection, you'd receive regular updates about reviewing opportunities.

  • As a reader, how much will I be paid?

    You decide get to decide the price of your review. You can change it each time for a new reviewing opportunity.

  • I don't see my question listed, what should I do?

    Write us a query at honestscribbles@gmail.com and we'd make sure to reach back with an answer.
